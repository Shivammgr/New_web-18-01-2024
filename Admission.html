While the test assesses the proficiency in various subjects, it also reveals the child's problem-solving skills.

Registration

At the time of registration, the following documents along with registration fee should be submitted to the School Office. Filled in form should be submitted in the office along with

  • Latest coloured passport size photograph of the student.
  • Photograph of the parent with the child in [5cm x3.5cm]
  • Photocopy of Birth Certificate (attested).
  • Original Medical certificate of student.
  • Photocopy of the result of the previous class (attested).
  • In case the registration form is filled by guardians other than parents, it should be clearly indicated
  • Address Proof: -Pan card/Driving licence/Electricity Bill/ Aadhar Card.
  • Admissions up to class 9th will be on the basis of the entrance exam.
  • The admission would be confirmed only after the personal interview.
  • Only meritorious students will be considered for midterm admission on grounds of transfer or change of school.
  • The school reserves the right to admit a student in the appropriate class after examining the student. Even after the admission is granted the student can either be adjusted in a lower or higher class, whichever the case may be, depending upon the student's ability at the discretion of the authorities.
  • Admission is strictly subject to fulfilling of all conditions and submission of requisite documents. TC from the previous school must be submitted within 15 days after the commencement of the academic session.
  • Academic Session fee will be collected on monthly basis. Details can be taken from the fee counter.
  • Note: The school reserves the right to revise the fee as determined by the School Board of Management.
  • The fee revision will be as per the norms of Bihar Govt. 7% annually. The school management can increase the transport fee with the increase in fuel rates.
